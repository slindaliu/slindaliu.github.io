Dr. Liu is engaged in the research of artificial intelligence algorithms and their chips, and is committed to realizing energy-efficient and scalable intelligent processing chips and systems through cross-level collaborative optimization of circuits, architectures and algorithms. He has published more than 20 SCI/EI papers in related fields, obtained 2 authorized patents, and presided over the National Natural Science Youth Foundation, Shanghai Natural Science Foundation, ASIC and System State Key Laboratory project. As a research backbone, he participated in the National Natural Science Foundation project, joint fund project, JKW project, etc.
